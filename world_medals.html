Worldwide Medals

Visualisation World Map Medal Distribution by Country

Visualizing the distribution of Olympic medals by country is crucial to understanding each nation's athletic prowess. Our tool provides a comprehensive view of medal counts over the years, enabling users to explore trends and patterns.

The visualization will depict the number of medals won by countries over different time spans. By interacting with our tool, users can gain insights into the evolving dynamics of Olympic success.

To enhance user interaction, we've integrated features such as color-coded maps and interactive data sheets. Hovering over a country reveals detailed information, including total medal count and medal composition (gold, silver, bronze).

Furthermore, users can delve deeper into historical trends by clicking on countries to access year-by-year medal statistics. This layered approach enables a more nuanced analysis of sporting achievements over time.

Our aim is to create a visually appealing and user-friendly platform that facilitates exploration and understanding of Olympic medal data in a novel manner.
